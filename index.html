F.A.Q Accordion
  • What is Gelaro

    Gelaro is a platform that enable you to create an event online. This app will help you to find & compare any vendors you need just in your hand.

  • How to use it?

    You can find in your sidebar menu the button named "Create an event". Then choose your vendors. It's simple and easy!

  • What feature I can get?

    You can compare some vendors. Give credits to your vendors. Booking by one vendors.
